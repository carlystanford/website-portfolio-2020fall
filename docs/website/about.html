My name is Carly, I'm a senior at the University of Pittsburgh, and I love to
bake. I started baking when I was in middle school, and I was mostly drawn
to it because of my huge sweet tooth. I will choose something sweet over
something savory or salty 9 times out of 10, so my initial motivation for
baking was just the end product. I started simple, with box mixes
and pre-made doughs before learning how to make things from scratch.
Now, I not only enjoy the end product of baking, but the process as well. I
find it meditative to throw on a good playlist or podcast episode and get to
work mixing, kneading, or rolling. My favorite things to bake are those that
have simple techniques, but interesting ingredients or flavor profiles. Some
